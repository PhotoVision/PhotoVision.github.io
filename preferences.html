Submit or update preferences

Our world-class color techs can best match your vision when you provide the road map! And the best road maps include both preference images and written preferences.

  • Email used to create account*

  • Preference images (.png, .jpg)

    Upload 3-5 images that best exemplify the skin tones, density and contrast level you are striving to achieve. Pick images where your subject fills the majority of the frame.

  • Written preferences

    Describe your ideal overall image:  Examples:
    - Warm / cool / neutral
    - Dark + moody / light + airy
    - Details in shadows / shadows nearly black
    - Retain details in highlights / blown-out highlights OK
    - Avoid vague descriptions open to interpretation (i.e. "masculine," "romantic")
    - Avoid describing skin tones–rather, let your preference images speak to skin tones
